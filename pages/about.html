we are a small team of developers who are focused on building great products, not upselling you on features.

we know first-hand that remembering your team’s engineering standards is hard, and when it becomes the thing standing between your team and the work they love, something has gone wrong.

we wondered: what if you could add an automated layer of configurable, exhaustive review between the author and the reviewer?

knitpick is that layer. a review system that learns about your developers’ patterns, understands your company’s rules, and can help you achieve a more focused product.
our multi-layered AI pipeline filters out the noise so that every comment provided to your developers is worth reading (and so that we can offer you the best prices on the market).

we love developers, and we want to produce great products that make their lives easier.

if you'd like to learn more about the product and it's technical implementation, check out [docs] for more information.
